/ STRATEGY

How The Strategy Works

Confluence-based trend-following with volume + volatility filters. Every trade is gated by four independent checks.

STEP 01 📊

Data Feed

Bybit Mainnet · 5-min candles

  • 500 candles per asset
  • BTC perp · XAUT spot · AGLD spot
›
STEP 02 📈

Indicators

Computed in real time

  • EMA (fast / slow)
  • RSI (14)
  • ATR (14)
  • Volume MA (20)
  • EMA (200) trend filter
›
STEP 03 ⚙️

Signal Engine

All 4 conditions must be TRUE

  • EMA fast > EMA slow (crossover)
  • RSI(14) < 65 (not overbought)
  • Volume > 20-period Volume MA
  • Price > EMA(200) (uptrend only)
›
STEP 04 🎯

Execution

Bybit Testnet · market orders

  • Stop Loss = Entry - 1.5 × ATR
  • Take Profit = Entry + 3.0 × ATR
›
STEP 05 🔄

Auto-Optimizer

Triggers every 20 trades

  • Grid search across parameter space
  • 70/30 train/test split
  • Prevents overfitting
